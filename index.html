What's Up !!

I'm Trai Lynne Compton

and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer

I am what I like to consider an introverted nerd. I love computers and numbers as well as writing in my journals. I started my journey into tech at a pretty young age. I have always had a passion for STEM subjects. I would take apart my electronic toys to figure out how they work so I can put them back together. After taking an elective in middle school to learn the basics of web development, I began to customize my MySpace profile instead of using premade themes. Then I became an adult... After I graduated high school, I entered the telecommunications career field.

It did not take long for me to realize that answering the phones all day was not fulfilling. I was determined to find a way to further my tech skills in order to enter a career field I actually enjoy. I enrolled in Skillcrush for Front-End Web Development. Shortly after completing the course, I took it a step further and enrolled in Flatiron School for Full-Stack Web Development. Now I’ve made the switch to Full-Stack Development. With a background in customer service and technical support, I bring skills in teamwork, attentiveness, tenacity, and communication.
